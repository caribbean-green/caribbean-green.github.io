ABOUT US

Caribbean Green Infrastructure is a strategic partnership formed by three leading construction and engineering firms: PC Mejía, Coninsa, and Owen Steel. With over 50 years of combined experience in infrastructure development, we have united our expertise to bring world-class construction solutions to Guyana and the wider Caribbean region.

Founded on a shared commitment to engineering excellence, sustainable infrastructure, and project delivery at scale, Caribbean Green Infrastructure was created to meet the growing demand for reliable, future-ready developments. Our firm specializes in large-scale construction projects across four key sectors: hospitality and commercial, healthcare facilities, government and institutional buildings, and residential real estate.

From engineering and design to procurement, construction, and project financing, we operate across every stage of the project lifecycle. Our integrated approach allows us to deliver high-impact projects that are not only on time and on budget but built to last.

With a multidisciplinary team and the combined strengths of our founding companies, we ensure every project meets international standards of quality, efficiency, and sustainability. Whether it’s building a state-of-the-art hotel, a medical complex, or a government facility, we are committed to transforming Guyana’s infrastructure with purpose and precision.

As Guyana’s infrastructure sector experiences rapid growth, Caribbean Green Infrastructure is proud to play a central role in shaping its future—delivering innovative construction solutions that support economic development and improve quality of life across the region.
