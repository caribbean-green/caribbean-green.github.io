ABOUT US

Caribbean Green Infrastructure is a strategic partnership formed by three leading construction and engineering firms: PC Mejía, Coninsa, and Owen Steel. With over 50 years of combined experience in infrastructure development, we have united our expertise to bring world-class construction solutions to Guyana and the wider Caribbean region.

Founded on a shared commitment to engineering excellence, sustainable infrastructure, and project delivery at scale, our mission is to meet the growing demand for reliable, future-ready developments. CGI specializes in delivering large-scale projects that enhance communities and support long-term growth.
