ABOUT US

Caribbean Green Infrastructure is a strategic partnership formed by three leading construction and engineering firms: PC Mejía, Coninsa, and Owen Steel. With over 50 years of combined experience in infrastructure development, we have united our expertise to bring world-class construction solutions to Guyana and the wider Caribbean region.

Founded on a shared commitment to engineering excellence, sustainable infrastructure, and project delivery at scale, we specialize in hospitality, healthcare, government, and residential sectors. From engineering and design to procurement, construction, and project financing, we operate across every stage of the lifecycle. Whether it’s building a state-of-the-art hotel or a government facility, we deliver purposefully and precisely.
